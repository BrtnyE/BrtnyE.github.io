Core Business- "manufacturing"...
 Manufacturing forms the core activity of this company. After more than 26 years’ experience in the industry, the
company consider themselves as one of the exceptional integrated factories in the industry. The factory prides itself
on having in-house knitting, dyeing, designing, tailoring and cutting, sewing, finishing and distribution facilities. The
ability to control these activities is their competitive advantage amongst the company competitors.

Downstream distribution network "pull strategy"
 The rationale for their design and market driven strategy is to generate demand for the manufacturing business.
Since the mid-80s, this company has consciously increased its design and marketing expertise.
